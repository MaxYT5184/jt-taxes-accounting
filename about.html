About JT Tax & Accounting

Your trusted partner for financial success with over a decade of experience serving Scranton, PA.

Our Story

Founded in Scranton, JT Tax & Accounting began with a single goal: to provide dependable, personalized financial services for individuals and small businesses. Over the past 10 years, we’ve grown into a trusted local firm known for accuracy, integrity, and exceptional client service.

Our team works closely with clients, ensuring every financial decision is informed and strategically planned. From tax preparation to bookkeeping, our approach is tailored to meet your unique goals.

Our Mission

Empowering clients with clear, accurate financial guidance. We aim to reduce tax liabilities, simplify accounting, and foster long-term financial success for individuals and businesses alike.

Why Choose Us?

  • 10+ Years of Expertise: Deep understanding of tax laws and accounting principles.
  • Personalized Service: Every client receives individual attention and tailored solutions.
  • Local Knowledge: We understand Scranton’s business and tax landscape.
  • Year-Round Support: Assistance extends beyond tax season.
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees; competitive rates for our services.
