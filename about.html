About JT Tax & Accounting

Your trusted partner for financial success with 10+ years of experience

Our Story

JT Tax & Accounting was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, personalized financial services to individuals and small businesses in Scranton and surrounding areas. With over 10 years of experience in tax preparation and accounting, we've built a reputation for accuracy, integrity, and personalized service.

We believe that everyone deserves access to professional financial guidance, regardless of the size of their business. Our approach is built on understanding each client's unique situation and providing tailored solutions that meet their specific needs and goals.

Our Mission

To empower our clients with accurate financial information, strategic guidance, and personalized service that helps them make informed decisions, minimize tax liabilities, and achieve long-term financial success.

Why Choose Us?

  • 10+ Years of Experience: Extensive knowledge in tax laws and accounting practices
  • Personalized Service: We take time to understand your unique financial situation
  • Local Expertise: We understand Scranton's business environment and tax requirements
  • Year-Round Support: We're here for you beyond tax season
  • Affordable Pricing: Competitive rates with transparent pricing
