Our Story

JT Taxs & Accounting was founded in 2008 by John Thompson with a simple mission: to provide reliable, personalized financial services to individuals and small businesses in our community. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted firm serving hundreds of clients annually.

We believe that everyone deserves access to professional financial guidance, regardless of the size of their business. Our team is committed to building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and helping them achieve their financial goals.

Our Mission

To empower our clients with accurate financial information, strategic guidance, and personalized service that helps them make informed decisions, minimize tax liabilities, and achieve long-term financial success.
