Our Professional Services

Comprehensive financial solutions for individuals and businesses in Scranton, PA

Tax Services

We provide comprehensive tax preparation and planning services to ensure compliance and maximize your refunds. With 10+ years of experience, we stay current with changing tax laws to provide you with the most advantageous filing strategies.

Individual Tax Preparation

Complete preparation of federal and state income tax returns for individuals, including itemized deductions, investment income, rental properties, and retirement income.

Business Tax Preparation

Tax return preparation for corporations, partnerships, LLCs, and sole proprietorships, including all necessary schedules and forms for Pennsylvania and federal requirements.

Tax Planning & Strategy

Proactive tax planning to minimize liabilities and maximize savings throughout the year, not just at tax time. We help you make tax-smart financial decisions.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Keep your financial records accurate and up-to-date with our professional accounting services. We help you maintain organized financial records for better business decisions.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Regular maintenance of your financial records including accounts payable/receivable, bank reconciliations, and financial statement preparation.

Payroll Processing

Complete payroll services including paycheck processing, tax withholding, and payroll tax filings for Pennsylvania businesses.

Financial Statement Preparation

Preparation of balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements for better business decision-making and loan applications.

Business Advisory Services

Strategic guidance to help your business grow and prosper. We provide the financial insight you need to make informed decisions.

  • Business Formation: Guidance on choosing the right business structure (LLC, Corporation, Partnership) and assistance with Pennsylvania business registration
  • Budgeting & Forecasting: Development of realistic budgets and financial forecasts to guide your business planning
  • Cash Flow Management: Analysis and strategies to improve cash flow and working capital management
  • QuickBooks Setup & Training: Professional setup and training for QuickBooks accounting software
