How to Prepare for Your First Visit

Essential Documents for Tax Preparation

To make your tax preparation process smooth and efficient, please gather these important documents before your appointment:

1
W-2 forms from all employers
2
1099 forms for freelance/contract work
3
Investment income statements (1099-DIV, 1099-INT)
4
Mortgage interest statements (Form 1098)
5
Property tax receipts for Pennsylvania
6
Charitable contribution receipts
7
Business expense records if self-employed
8
Last year's tax return for reference

Don't worry if you're missing some documents - we can help you obtain duplicates or work with what you have available.
